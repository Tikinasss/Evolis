## SCRIPT NOTES FOR VIDEO PRODUCTION

### **Pacing & Timing:**
- Total length: ~4:35 minutes
- Break up sections with visuals/animations every 4-5 seconds
- Use text overlays for key points
- Include real-world footage or illustrations of featured concepts

### **Visual Recommendations:**
1. **Opening (0:00-0:15):** Animated business charts showing decline, then turnaround
2. **Problem (0:15-0:45):** Real entrepreneur struggles, time-lapse of slow consulting process
3. **Solution (0:45-1:30):** Step-by-step animated process showing data input → AI analysis → results
4. **Features (1:30-2:20):** Feature card animations, dashboard screen recording, PDF sample
5. **Why Evolis (2:20-3:00):** Side-by-side comparison graphics, testimonial-style quotes
6. **Use Cases (3:00-3:30):** Three brief story scenarios with relevant background footage
7. **Tech (3:30-4:00):** Architecture diagram, server/database visualization
8. **Future (4:00-4:20):** Roadmap timeline with feature previews
9. **CTA (4:20-4:30):** Website walkthrough, signup flow
10. **Closing (4:30-4:35):** Success stories montage

### **Tone Guidance:**
- **Professional but accessible** — avoid jargon overload
- **Empathetic to pain points** — acknowledge real business struggles
- **Confident in solution** — speak with authority about AI capabilities
- **Inspiring** — leave viewers feeling hopeful about recovery

### **Background Music Suggestions:**
- Opening: Upbeat, modern, tech-forward instrumental
- Problem section: Slight tension, dramatic urgency
- Solution section: Rising, inspirational crescendo
- Features: Clear, contemporary background music
- Closing: Uplifting, positive resolution

### **Call-to-Action Variations (Depending on Goal):**
1. **Sign-up focus:** "Start your free diagnostic today at evolis.ai"
2. **Demo focus:** "Watch a live demo on our website"
3. **Enterprise focus:** "Book a personalized consultation with our team"
4. **Lead gen focus:** "Get your free business health report now"
